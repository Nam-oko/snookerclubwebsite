Snooker Tips and Tricks

Improve Your Stance

One of the key factors in snooker is having a solid stance. Ensure that your feet are shoulder-width apart, bend your knees slightly, and keep your body relaxed. This will provide stability and help you deliver a smooth stroke.

Master Cue Ball Control

Developing good cue ball control is essential in snooker. Practice various shots to understand how different angles and spins affect the cue ball's path. Focus on positioning the cue ball for your next shot and plan ahead.

Break Building Techniques

Building a break requires careful planning and execution. Start with easy pots and gradually work your way to more challenging shots. Aim to keep the cue ball under control and create opportunities for consecutive potting.
